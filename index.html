The Psychic Game

A JavaScript game that will keep you preoccupied for minutes (maybe one minute)

Instructions:

  • Guess what letter I'm thinking of by pressing any letter on your keyboard
  • You will get 10 guesses each round to try to match my letter
  • If you correctly match my letter within 10 tries, you win!
  • If you are unable to correctly guess my letter within 10 choices, you lose!

    • -----You may begin the game by pressing your first letter guess!-----
